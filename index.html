In 2016, we created HOSS GLOBAL with a custom-tailored business consulting experience that would provide outstanding service to emerging companies in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA).

In early 2017, we began expanding our network resources by collaborating with strategic service providers across the US, UK, Europe, and Asia to acquire interests in retail and hospitality.

We offer a full range of support services to help our clients achieve their business goals and sustain growth.
